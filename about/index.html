About Beth Miller

Who I Am

My name is Beth Miller, and I am licensed as a Clinical Social Worker in North Carolina. I support individuals and families to navigate through difficult times in a safe, therapeutic space. I've been providing therapy for over 10 years in the Asheville area, but it wasn't always this way.

I have had many different roles over the past 15 years. I worked with homeless teenagers, foster children and parents, foreign exchange students, and have also provided in-home therapy and in-school services for high needs children and families. Ultimately, I was able to find the best balance of my own passions and self care while working in private practice. Here I provide a safe space for individuals and families where they can discover who they truly are, what their needs are, and how to decide the most successful path for themselves.

My Approach

The main goal of spending time in a therapeutic space with me is to find empowerment and clarity. Ideally this supports positive change with daily functioning, personal identity, relationships, emotional regulation, and trauma responses.

I have been through many trainings, but what I have learned from real life therapy is that everyone is an individual with specific desires, baggage, dislikes, and goals. Each client is their own guide with my support.

I believe in empowering emotional development and cognitive awareness. I dive into the ideas about autonomy and active choices. Noticing what things drive us, trigger us, and motivate us can be highly impactful. Taking an inventory of negative messages such as EMDR messages and recurring thoughts can be helpful to decide what to do moving forward.

Please reach out today if you would like to see if it would be a good fit to work together.
